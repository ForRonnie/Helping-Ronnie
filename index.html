Thanks to Heroes Like You, Ronnie's Treatment is Fully Funded!

Amazing news! Thanks to generous donors, Ronnie's heartworm treatment has been fully funded! Your compassion made this possible.

But Ronnie's story doesn't end here - it's just beginning. His journey represents hope for thousands of other dogs waiting in shelters.

Time is running out for dogs just like Ronnie. Right now, in shelters across America, thousands of perfect dogs are watching days pass by, not knowing if their future holds a loving home or euthanasia.

Every single day, wonderful dogs with loyal hearts and loving spirits face euthanasia simply because shelters run out of space and resources. This is the reality that motivated us to create the Ronnie Legacy Fund.

Your donation today can literally save a life tomorrow.

In Memory of Those Who Never Made It Out

We honor the dogs who ran out of time before finding their forever homes. Their stories fuel our mission to create a world where every dog gets the chance they deserve.

Grady

A 3-year-old hugger who retrieved toys with gentle care. Grady just needed a quiet home and a loving adopter who never made it to the shelter before euthanasia won the tragic race.

Betty

Dumped at a city dog park at just one year old. Betty's wiggly, eager-to-learn spirit waited for a quiet home and loving adopter who never arrived before her time ran out.

Kaylee

Found as a stray, this playful 2-year-old loved one-on-one attention. Kaylee just needed a quiet home away from the chaotic shelter, but euthanasia claimed her before that loving adopter appeared.
